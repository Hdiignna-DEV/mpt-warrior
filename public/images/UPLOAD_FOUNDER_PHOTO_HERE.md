# Upload Founder Photo

## Instructions:
1. Prepare founder photo:
   - **Recommended size**: 400x400px or larger (square ratio)
   - **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - **File size**: < 500KB for optimal loading
   - **Style**: Professional headshot with neutral background

2. Rename file to: `founder-photo.jpg` (or `.png`)

3. Upload to this folder: `/public/images/`

4. Delete this file after upload

## Quick Photo Tips:
- Good lighting (natural light preferred)
- Clear face, slight smile
- Professional attire
- Solid or blurred background
- Eye level camera angle

The photo will appear in:
- About page header
- Email signatures (future)
- Admin profile (future)
